Overview

The Gazo (plural: Gazos) is one of the seven core species of Glaggle native to the planet Gluborg. Distinctly recognizable by their deep blue coloration and perfectly spherical, uniform biological structure, Gazos are renowned across the system for their high emotional and physiological stability.

In modern Gluborgian society, Gazos primarily inhabit the Prime Latitudes—the most resource-rich, highly developed, and industrialized regions of the planet. To human sociologists, the Gazo territories are frequently compared to Earth's highly developed northern hemispheres or affluent nations. Because of their concentrated wealth, advanced infrastructure, and stable environments, the Gazos have naturally assumed the roles of planetary historians, tech developers, and interstellar diplomats.

Background & Culture

Following The Great Split, the ancestors of modern Gazos migrated toward the most temperate and resource-dense sectors of Gluborg. Free from the harsh survival conditions faced by the frontier-dwelling Glaabers or the migratory necessity of the Cheerers, Gazos were able to focus entirely on intellectual and cultural pursuits.

Gazo culture is heavily centered around the preservation of knowledge and maintaining planetary order. They are the sole architects and administrators of the Gazo-wiki, utilizing it to compile the histories of all other Glag species. When humanity first made contact with Gluborg (PRF-230K), it was the Gazo leadership who negotiated the historic non-interference treaties, famously mastering the English language faster than any other Glaggle variant.

Economy and Industry

The Gazo economy is overwhelmingly post-industrial, focusing on high technology, archival data storage, and interstellar trade. Because they reside in the wealthiest sectors of Gluborg, they rely heavily on trade with the agricultural Gembos for sustenance, and the metropolitan Glaggles (Standard) for mass-manufactured goods.

Trivia

  • Despite their inherent blue coloration, the official state color of the Gazo High Council is actually a deep, muted silver.
  • Gazos are the only species on Gluborg that undergo specialized "biological stabilization" treatments, ensuring they rarely experience extreme emotional volatility.
  • Within the Gazo-wiki network, only verified Gazos possess the administrative privileges required to edit core database articles, hence the persistent "Protected" status of most pages.
  • A common stereotype among the other six species is that Gazos are overly bureaucratic and "too perfectly round."
