Gembos

Gembos
Coloration: Red
Average Size: 1 meter (diameter), dense build
Primary Roles: Construction, Agriculture, Manufacturing
Temperament: Hardworking / Down-to-earth
"A blueprint is just a dream on paper until a Gembo gets their hands dirty."
— Common Gembo proverb

Overview

Gembos are a robust, red-hued species of Glag. If the Gazos are the brains of planetary industry and the Gigglers are the spirit, the Gembos are undoubtedly the muscle. They are the true backbone of Gluborg, doing the heavy lifting and hard work that literally keeps our world together. From raising cattle to laying the foundations of Glaggleland, nothing on this planet gets built, grown, or manufactured without a Gembo's touch.

Labor and Manufacturing

Gembos possess an incredible work ethic and physical stamina. They are the premier construction workers and manufacturers of Gluborg. When Gazos invent new technologies, it is the Gembos who figure out how to physically forge the parts and assemble them on a massive scale. Their manufacturing techniques are unmatched, blending raw strength with a surprising eye for durable craftsmanship.

Beyond the cities, Gembos thrive as ranchers and agriculturalists. They manage the vast farms that feed the planetary population, wrangling native livestock and tending to massive crops. Because they are always working with their hands, a Gembo is easily recognizable by their rugged texture and calloused grip.

Connection to Nature

Despite being our greatest builders and industrialists, Gembos have a profound and deeply rooted love for nature. They believe that true construction works with the environment, not against it. A Gembo will refuse to clear-cut a forest for a new factory, instead engineering a way to weave the building into the existing landscape. They are the reason Gluborg remains a lush, green paradise even as our civilization advances and trades with the human empire.

Trivia

  • A Gembo's red coloration actually grows brighter and more vibrant when they are engaged in heavy physical labor.
  • They are notoriously early risers. Most Gembos have already completed half a day's work by the time a Giggler has finished their morning stretching.
  • While they respect Gazos for our organizational skills, they often tease us for having "soft hands" and spending too much time at our clipboards.
  • Gembos hold an annual weight-pulling competition during the planetary harvest festival, which draws massive crowds of cheering Glags.
