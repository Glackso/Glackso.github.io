"Tree tall. Fruit warm. Me reach."

Overview

Glaabers are a massive, orange-hued species of Glag. Measuring a staggering 5 meters in diameter, they are exactly five times the size of a standard Glag. They primarily inhabit the dense, towering forests of Gluborg, using their incredible height to reach the canopy and harvest the highly nutritious solar fruits that grow there. While their physical stature is intimidating, Glaabers are notoriously gentle, docile, and completely devoid of aggression.

Intelligence and Development

From an evolutionary and sociological standpoint, Glaabers are currently the least advanced species on the planet. They possess a very basic brain structure and the lowest literacy rate of any Glag. Their vocabulary is largely limited to simple nouns and verbs. As Gazo historians, we theorize that because their sheer size and strength naturally solved all of their survival problems, their brains simply never needed to evolve complex problem-solving skills.

The Manipulation Factor

Because they possess such a simple worldview, Glaabers are incredibly trusting. As a matter of objective record, they are highly manipulatable. A Glaaber will happily trade a month's worth of rare, valuable solar fruit for a single, shiny human bottle cap because they think it looks "pretty."

Naturally, one might assume that opportunistic individuals would take advantage of this. Officially, the Kepler-Gluborg Accords strictly prohibit the economic exploitation of Glaabers. Unofficially... Gazo administrators have had to establish heavy zoning regulations around the solar fruit groves to keep less scrupulous human merchants (and certain prank-loving Cheerers) from tricking the Glaabers into giving away their harvests for free.

Trivia

  • Due to their size, a Glaaber's footsteps can be felt vibrating through the ground up to a mile away.
  • When a Glaaber meets a Giggler, they often try to pick them up and treat them like a glowing yellow pet.
  • They view the 10-meter-wide Great Glag (the President of Glaggleland) with absolute awe, often mistaking him for a very large, talking sun.
  • Despite our best efforts to teach them how to use Gazowiki, most Glaabers just try to eat the computer monitors.
