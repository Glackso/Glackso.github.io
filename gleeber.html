Overview

Gleebers are a purple-hued, land-dwelling species of Glag. They are universally recognized across Gluborg for two things: their sprawling suburban habitats and their unprecedented, almost legendary levels of laziness. A Gleeber approaches every aspect of life with a deeply ingrained sense of apathy. To a Gleeber, there is no emergency so great that it cannot be solved after a quick nap on a concrete ramp.

Suburban Life and "Rad" Culture

Gleebers primarily reside in the quiet, paved suburban areas of Gluborg. For a long time, their daily routine consisted of slowly rolling down gentle hills and lounging on the sidewalks. However, everything changed when Ambassador Elias Vance's human crews arrived and shared their archives of late-20th-century extreme sports.

Upon discovering human concepts like skateboarding, BMX, inline skating, and anything generally described as "Rad," the Gleebers became instantly and permanently obsessed. Today, "Rad" culture is the singular thing that motivates a Gleeber to move. However, because they are still inherently lazy, they perform these extreme sports with a bizarrely relaxed, low-energy style. A Gleeber will execute a mathematically perfect 900-degree mid-air rotation while looking completely bored and half-asleep.

Suburban Planning

As Gazos, we find the Gleeber work ethic highly illogical. They were initially tasked with suburban zoning and civil engineering, but they immediately repurposed all their assigned resources to build massive concrete bowls, half-pipes, and inexplicably smooth handrails. Despite sleeping for roughly 16 hours a day and spending the rest of their time trying to perfect their "kickflips," the suburban infrastructure they manage is surprisingly sturdy—mostly because they need the concrete to be perfectly smooth for their wheels.

Trivia

  • In Gleeber culture, the word "yo" can mean hello, goodbye, yes, no, or "watch this trick," depending entirely on the subtle inflection used.
  • Many Gleebers have adopted the human custom of wearing backward baseball caps, trading valuable space-moss to human crews just to acquire them.
  • During the planetary off-season, Gleebers will purposefully sneak into Gloober territory to drain their water parks so they can skateboard in the massive fiberglass tubes.
  • If you challenge a Gleeber to a race on foot, they will decline. If you challenge them to a race on a skateboard, they will effortlessly defeat you without ever opening their eyes all the way.
