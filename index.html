Gluborg (known to humanity under the star chart designation PRF-230K) is a planetary body primarily inhabited by sphere-shaped organisms. Due to the rapid expansion of human space exploration, the planet and its inhabitants are strictly protected under a universal space treaty designed to maintain peaceful relations with non-human systems.

The Glaggles

The dominant lifeforms on Gluborg are collectively referred to as Glaggles. Biologically, they are highly unusual sphere-shaped organisms. Despite their alien physiology, Glaggles communicate fluently in English, which has become the universal standard language across known systems.

Gazos

Within the broader Glaggle classification, the Gazos are a specific, notable sub-species. They are distinctly blue in coloration and are known for being highly biologically and emotionally stable. Out of all the organisms on Gluborg, Gazos exhibit behaviors and social structures most similar to humans, making them the primary point of contact for diplomatic relations under the space treaty.
