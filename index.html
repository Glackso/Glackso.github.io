Greetings, Travelers!

Whether you are a fellow spherical inhabitant of Gluborg or a visiting human researcher from the Sol system, we welcome you to our archives. Following the monumental signing of the Interstellar Peace Coalition Treaty, humanity and the Glaggles have enjoyed an unprecedented era of cooperation and shared knowledge.

Because the English language has been adopted as the universal standard, we Gazos have taken it upon ourselves to translate and compile the entirety of our planet's history, biology, and culture into this accessible network. We deeply thank humanity for their peaceful approach to PRF-230K, and we hope this database serves as a bridge between our two worlds.
