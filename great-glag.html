Title: President of Glaggleland
Size: 10 meters (diameter)
Estimated Age: Pre-Proto-Gazoid (Allegedly)
Dietary Intake: Astronomical
"Wisdom is like a good space-melon. It takes time to grow, it is best shared with friends, and I would very much like to eat one right now."
— The Great Glag, during the 2088 State of Gluborg Address

Overview

As the objective record-keepers of Gluborg, we Gazos rely heavily on empirical data. However, when it comes to The Great Glag, the colossal President of Glaggleland, we are forced to simply document the planetary consensus, no matter how biologically improbable it seems. Measuring a staggering 10 meters in diameter, The Great Glag is a figure of immense wisdom, unparalleled authority, and an absolutely terrifying appetite.

Diet and Sustenance

To maintain a 10-meter-wide spherical body, The Great Glag eats a LOT. His daily caloric intake requires the dedicated labor of several massive Gembo agricultural sectors. Whether it is massive bushels of solar fruit harvested by Glaabers or imported human delicacies brought by Ambassador Vance’s trade fleets, The Great Glag is almost always eating. Despite this, he remains remarkably articulate and dispenses profound planetary guidance between bites.

Mythology: Peripheral Glag

According to Giggler theology and planetary folklore, The Great Glag is not a normal biological entity. He is widely believed to be the direct offspring of Peripheral Glag, the omnipresent, all-seeing Glag deity that theoretically encompasses the edges of the universe. Because of this divine lineage, the majority of the planet believes that The Great Glag actually existed before the Proto-Gazoids, calmly waiting for the Great Split to happen so he would finally have citizens to lead.

The DNA Controversy

Naturally, as scientists and historians, we Gazos found the claim that a living being predates our common ancestor to be highly illogical. In the year 2091, a team of top Gazo biologists respectfully requested a standard epidermal swab of The Great Glag to analyze his DNA and establish a factual timeline.

The backlash was immediate and severe. Every single species heavily disapproved of our attempt to "debunk" their beloved leader. The Gigglers refused to speak to us, the Gembos went on strike, the Gleebers temporarily stopped skateboarding out of protest, and the Cheerers deployed so much chaotic confetti into the Gazo archives that we were forced to officially apologize and abandon the research entirely. His true genetic origin remains an undocumented mystery.

Trivia

  • When The Great Glag laughs, the seismic tremors can be registered on Gazo fault-line monitors up to 50 miles away.
  • He holds the intergalactic record for the most human pizzas consumed in a single sitting (the exact number is classified by the Kepler-Gluborg Accords to prevent a human panic).
  • Despite his immense size, he is incredibly soft to the touch, which the Cheerers exploit by frequently using him as a giant, 10-meter bouncy castle. He does not seem to mind.
  • His official presidential podium is actually just a reinforced, hollowed-out asteroid provided by human engineers.
