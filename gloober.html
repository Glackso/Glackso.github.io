Overview

Gloobers are a green-hued species of Glag known for their deep, natural attachment to water and the environment. Easily the most supportive and nurturing of all the Glag sub-species, Gloobers act as the emotional anchors for many of their peers. While the Cheerers provide high-energy joy and the Gigglers offer visionary optimism, Gloobers provide a calm, steady stream of comfort and encouragement.

Water Parks and Recreation

Because of their affinity for aquatics, Gloobers are the undisputed masters of water-based recreation on Gluborg. They have engineered and constructed more water parks than any other species in the galaxy. These are not just standard pools; Gloober water parks are massive, sprawling ecosystems that perfectly blend natural waterfalls, bioluminescent kelp, and high-speed hydro-tubes.

Their parks serve as both recreational hubs and natural preserves. When human trade ships land, human crews are frequently granted shore leave specifically to visit Gloober water parks, which are widely considered a marvel of intergalactic engineering and natural beauty.

Society and Ecology

Gloobers are deeply empathetic and supportive. If a Gazo is stressed over an organizational chart, or a Giggler is overwhelmed by future possibilities, a Gloober is usually the first to step in, offer a listening ear, and suggest a relaxing float down a lazy river. They work closely with the Gembos to ensure that Gluborg's natural aquifers and rivers remain unpolluted and free-flowing.

Trivia

  • A Gloober's green coloration is directly tied to their hydration levels. They must soak in fresh water daily to maintain their vibrant hue.
  • They invented the "Glag-Splash," a planetary diving technique that maximizes water displacement (similar to a human cannonball).
  • Despite their calm demeanor, Gloobers are fiercely competitive when it comes to designing the tallest and fastest waterslides.
  • They share a strong bond with the aquatic Gleebers, often collaborating on underwater architecture (and sharing tips on how to build waterproof skateboards).
