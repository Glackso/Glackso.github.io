Cheerers

Cheerers
Coloration: Pink (with multi-colored accents)
Average Size: 1 meter (diameter)
Primary Roles: Morale Officers, Biological Jesters
Temperament: Chaotic / Empathic
"Why walk quietly when you can arrive in an explosion of brightly colored paper products?"
— Overheard in a Glaggleland plaza

Overview

Cheerers are a bright pink species of Glag, widely considered the emotional core of our society. They possess highly tuned empathic abilities that naturally elevate the moods of those around them, keeping planetary morale incredibly high. Their most distinct physical trait is their "confetti hair"—a fascinating evolutionary mutation that constantly generates and sheds colorful, paper-like flakes. The Cheerers absolutely love this mutation, wholly embracing their role as the biological jesters of Gluborg.

The Chaos Factor and Population

While their energy is infectious, Cheerers are also agents of pure, unadulterated chaos. They act on impulse, love a good prank, and possess almost zero volume control. Unfortunately, this chaotic lifestyle comes at a cost. Cheerers currently have the lowest population of all seven Glag sub-species, primarily because their reckless antics often lead to highly avoidable accidents. They simply lack a basic sense of self-preservation when a joke or a party is on the line.

Because of this, planetary leaders—including The Great Glag and several Gazo efficiency boards—are currently attempting to implement behavioral improvement programs. The goal is to teach Cheerers basic safety and "situational awareness" to help boost their population over time. However, as any Gazo who has tried to lecture a Cheerer will tell you, this is going to take a very, very long time.

Habitat and Social Behavior

Cheerers refuse to live in isolation. They are extreme extroverts who always migrate to the most densely populated areas of Gluborg. If a new city or trade hub opens up, you can expect a flock of Cheerers to move in shortly after. They feed off the kinetic energy of crowds, making them a staple in places like Glaggleland, where they can entertain maximum audiences simultaneously.

Trivia

  • The "confetti" they shed is 100% biodegradable, but it is still a massive headache for Gazo street-sweepers who prefer perfectly clean walkways.
  • When a Cheerer sneezes, it triggers a specialized reflex that fires a concentrated burst of confetti up to 3 meters away.
  • Despite their lack of focus, they have an uncanny ability to know exactly who in a room is having the worst day, immediately targeting them with aggressive positivity.
  • Human trade crews often try to hire Cheerers as ship mascots for long voyages, though the Kepler-Gluborg Accords strictly regulate how much "chaos" is legally allowed on a starship.
