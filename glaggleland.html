Overview

Glaggleland is the undisputed cultural, economic, and recreational capital of Gluborg. Acting as a massive melting pot for all seven Glag sub-species, it is a sprawling, neon-lit metropolis of opportunity. Unlike human nations which have long, complex histories of tariffs, borders, and conflicts, Glaggleland was founded on a very simple premise: everyone should be allowed to live together, eat massive portions of food, and ride rollercoasters all day long.

Because of the Kepler-Gluborg Accords, the city operates as a completely free-trade zone. It is a beacon of intergalactic tourism, featuring more amusement parks, flashing lights, and theatrical shows per square mile than anywhere else in the known universe.

The Sector System

Because putting a highly aquatic Gloober next to a hyper-energetic Giggler is a logistical nightmare, Gazo urban planners and Gembo construction crews divided Glaggleland into specialized Sectors. This ensures that the city remains a unified melting pot while catering to the highly specific biological and recreational needs of every citizen:

  • The Joyous Core: The center of the city, absolutely packed with towering Ferris wheels, loop-de-loop coasters, and fireworks. This is the primary habitat of the Gigglers and Cheerers. It is incredibly loud, smells like cotton candy, and is always covered in a fresh layer of confetti.
  • The Concrete Canyons: A sprawling suburban sector specifically paved with ultra-smooth concrete. There are no stairs here—only ramps, half-pipes, and grind rails. It is effectively a city-sized skatepark where the Gleebers reside, slowly rolling from place to place.
  • The Aqua-District: Managed by the Gloobers, this sector is essentially a continuation of Splash-Borg. It features lazy rivers instead of sidewalks and massive wave pools instead of public parks.
  • The Canopy Commons: Designed for the giant, simple-minded Glaabers. The buildings here are incredibly large, very basic, and completely devoid of sharp edges. It features giant, warm solar-lamps that the Glaabers like to hug.
  • The Grid: The only quiet sector in the city. Perfectly rectangular, heavily air-conditioned, and completely devoid of fun. This is where the Gazos manage the city's taxes and the Gembos manufacture the rollercoaster parts.

The Presidential Center

Right in the exact geographic center of Glaggleland sits The Great Glag. Because he measures 10 meters across and eats constantly, the city's entire infrastructure was quite literally built in a massive circle around him. Several major highways exist solely to deliver human pizzas and solar fruit directly to his podium.

Trivia

  • There has never been a war or civil conflict in Glaggleland. The closest they ever came to a riot was when a Gazo accidentally scheduled the Gembo weight-pulling contest on the same day as the Gleeber extreme sports finals.
  • Due to the sheer amount of neon lights and fireworks in The Joyous Core, Glaggleland is the only city on Gluborg visible from deep space without a telescope.
  • Human tourists frequently compare the city to historical Earth locations like "Orlando" or "Las Vegas," though Gazos note that Glaggleland is significantly cleaner.
  • Gazo urban planners assigned to Glaggleland get an extra four weeks of mandatory vacation time to recover from the stress of managing Cheerer-induced traffic jams.
