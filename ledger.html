Overview

President Reginald P. Ledger is a standard, one-meter-wide blue Gazo and the current elected leader of the United Directorates of Gazoria. While he possesses no extraordinary physical traits or magical mutations, he is widely considered by Gazo historians to be the most structurally perfect politician in the history of Gluborg. His absolute mastery of logistics, spreadsheets, and municipal zoning has earned him the profound respect of the Gazo populace.

The United Directorates of Gazoria

Gazoria is the highly formalized, meticulously planned nation-state situated in Gluborg's northern temperate zone. The nation boasts a demographic makeup that is precisely 96.75% Gazo. It is an industrial and archival utopia characterized by perfectly gridded streets, predictable weather patterns (thanks to atmospheric stabilizers), and an utter lack of unscheduled events.

While Glaggleland to the south is a chaotic melting pot of all species, Gazoria is the quiet, humming engine room of the planet. Visitors from the Human Empire have often compared its societal structure to the ancient human nation of the United Kingdom, albeit with significantly less rain and considerably more clipboards.

Leadership and the Fifth Term

President Ledger is currently serving an unprecedented fifth consecutive term. Unlike Giggler politicians who rely on massive reserves of Vitamin J and charismatic foresight to win over crowds, Ledger wins elections through undeniable, irrefutable data. His campaigns do not feature rallies; instead, he simply mails every citizen a highly detailed line graph demonstrating the steady increase in their quality of life.

Under his administration, planetary factory output has increased by a mathematically perfect 14.2%, and the average wait time for a bureaucratic permit has been reduced to a mere forty-five seconds. Gazos find his dry, emotionless reading of annual budgets to be incredibly soothing.

Trivia

  • President Ledger personally holds the largest private collection of vintage, pre-First Contact human clipboards.
  • He once vetoed an inter-species infrastructure bill simply because the giggler diplomats submitted it using an unapproved, highly unprofessional font.
  • His presidential desk is famously completely empty at all times, as he processes and files every incoming document the exact second it is handed to him.
  • Cheerers are legally permitted to visit Gazoria, but President Ledger implemented a law requiring them to fill out Form 8B-Dash at the border to declare their exact confetti payload before entry.
