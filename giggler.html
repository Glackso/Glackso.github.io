Gigglers

Gigglers
Coloration: Yellow
Average Size: 1 meter (Except the President)
Primary Roles: Diplomats, Guides, Visionaries
Temperament: Joyous / Energetic
"To see a bright future is a biological gift, but to run toward it with a massive smile and absolutely no map—that is what makes us Gigglers!"
— John J. Gig, Signatory of the Kepler-Gluborg Accords

Overview

Gigglers are a bright yellow, highly intuitive species of Glag. Known for their boundless energy and joyous demeanor, they are the diplomats, visionaries, and spiritual guides of Gluborg. While all Glags naturally contain a certain baseline amount of Vitamin J (the biological compound responsible for feelings of joy and optimism), Gigglers are practically bursting with it. This high concentration gives them their sunny disposition and their unique ability to perceive and seek out a better future.

Society and Glaggleland

Because they are constantly seeking a brighter tomorrow, Gigglers are incredibly active and social. They are the primary founders of Glaggleland, the largest and most bustling metropolis on Gluborg. Glaggleland is a true melting pot; because of its welcoming atmosphere and prime location, you can find members of all seven Glag sub-species living and working there harmoniously.

The Great Glag

Gigglers, and by extension the city of Glaggleland, are presided over by a singular, awe-inspiring leader known as The Great Glag. While a standard Giggler measures about 1 meter in diameter, the President is an anomaly of nature. The Great Glag is roughly 10 times the size of a normal glag (measuring an astounding 10 meters across), making him twice as large as even the giant canopy-dwelling Glaabers. His massive size is said to be a physical manifestation of his immense wisdom and concentrated Vitamin J.

Abilities and Flaws

The Gigglers' primary ability is "future-seeking." They have an instinctual, almost prophetic intuition that allows them to sense the best possible outcomes for planetary decisions. This made John J. Gig the perfect candidate to negotiate the intergalactic treaty with the humans.

However, as we Gazos have rigorously documented, Gigglers have one major flaw: a severe lack of direction. Because they are so focused on the joyous end-goal, they often have no idea how to actually get there. They rely heavily on other species (like Gazos and Gembos) to map out the logical steps and build the infrastructure required to achieve their grand visions.

Trivia

  • Due to their extreme Vitamin J levels, a Giggler will literally bounce when they are excited.
  • The Great Glag rarely leaves the center of Glaggleland, as moving a 10-meter-wide Glag disrupts traffic.
  • Gigglers get along exceptionally well with the pink, chaos-loving Cheerers, much to the dismay of anyone trying to get a good night's sleep in Glaggleland.
  • Human traders often trade high-grade citrus fruits with Gigglers, as the color yellow is considered highly fashionable in Giggler culture.
