Overview

John J. Gig is a prominent yellow Giggler and the current Chief Diplomat of Gluborg. From an objective, historical standpoint, he is a highly competent civil servant who utilized his species' natural future-seeking abilities to secure planetary sovereignty during first contact with the Human Empire. However, to the rest of the Glag population, John J. Gig is treated as an absolute intergalactic superstar.

The Kepler-Gluborg Accords

In the late 21st century (human calendar year 2084), when the vast human expansion reached Kepler-774b, planetary tensions were briefly elevated. Utilizing his massive reserves of Vitamin J, John J. Gig perceived a highly beneficial future of mutual trade. He boldly approached the human representative, Ambassador Elias Vance, and successfully negotiated the Kepler-Gluborg Accords.

As Gazos, we must accurately note for the historical record that while John J. Gig provided the vision and the charisma for the treaty, he possessed the standard Giggler flaw of lacking direction. A team of twenty Gazo legal clerks had to actually draft, format, and spell-check the entire treaty before he signed it.

Celebrity Status and Public Hysteria

Following the treaty, the planetary reaction to John J. Gig became highly illogical. He is currently the most famous individual on Gluborg (second only to The Great Glag). Gigglers faint when he bounces into a room. Cheerers constantly follow him, deploying so much celebratory confetti that it constitutes a public hazard. Even the hardworking Gembos have carved massive, smiling statues of him into the cliffsides near Glaggleland.

There is currently a thriving, unregulated market for "J.J. Gig" merchandise. As archivists, we find the existence of John J. Gig lunchboxes, action figures, and human-style foam fingers to be incredibly distracting to the planetary workflow, though we dutifully catalog them anyway.

Trivia

  • John J. Gig and Ambassador Elias Vance remain close personal friends and reportedly meet annually to trade high-grade human citrus for premium space-moss.
  • He holds the record for the highest recorded concentration of Vitamin J in a standard 1-meter Glag.
  • Despite his wealth and fame, he frequently gets lost in his own administrative building and has to ask Gloober janitors for directions to his office.
  • He was once asked by the Gleebers to do a kickflip. He successfully saw the future of the trick, attempted it, and instantly sprained his diplomatic ankle.
